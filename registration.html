Registration

Abstract and poster submission

Submit an abstract for a contributed talk or a poster through EasyChair here.
The deadline for abstract and poster submissions is April 1st, 2021 at 11:59 PM CET.

Notification of acceptance will be sent by April 6th, 2021.

Fee waivers

There will be a special issue related to the Conference topic on the journal Life (IF 2.991). A 20% discount on the publication fee will be applied for the special issue.

20 fee waivers for Ph.D. students and young post-docs offered by MPDI Journals are available. To apply for the fee waiver, please send your abstract via the Easy Chair link in the Abstract submission section, and send an email with your CV and two lines of motivations to liph.unipd@gmail.com with subject "SMEEB 2021 Fee Waiver Request".

MDPI Journals will also sponsor a 200€ award for the best contributed talk and a 100€ award for the best poster.

Registration

Pre-registration is open here. The registration fee for online participation is:
Ph.D. students and Post-doc: 25 euros
Researchers, Professors: 50 euros
Non Academics: 75 euros
Master students: free
Registration will open soon with deadline April 16th, 2021.

Due to the current COVID-19 pandemic, attendance fee for participation in person will be communicated at a later date. We will contact those who have pre-registered for in person participation.
